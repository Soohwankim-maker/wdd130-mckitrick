Assassin’s Creed Shadows is a 2025 open-world action-adventure set in feudal Japan, featuring two playable characters: Naoe, a stealthy shinobi assassin, and Yasuke, a powerful African samurai. Blending refined stealth mechanics, intense sword combat, and dynamic seasonal environments, the game lets players switch between silent infiltration and direct confrontation. With stunning visuals, a reactive world, and a dual-protagonist narrative, Shadows marks a return to classic Assassin’s Creed roots while introducing fresh mechanics that make it one of the most ambitious and polished entries in the series

Elden Ring is an expansive, dark fantasy action RPG developed by FromSoftware. it blends intense combat, deep lore, and open-world exploration in a way that feels both brutal and beautiful. Elden Ring is set in The Lands Between, a mysterious and shattered realm once ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal. After the destruction of the Elden Ring and the scattering of its shards (Great Runes), the world is plunged into chaos. Demigods, each corrupted by power, vie for control. You play as a Tarnished, an exile summoned back to the Lands Between to rise, fight, and ultimately become the Elden Lord. The narrative is cryptic and environmental, revealed through item descriptions, NPC dialogue, and atmospheric storytelling — a signature FromSoftware style.

God of War Ragnarök is a 2022 action-adventure game developed by Santa Monica Studio, continuing Kratos and Atreus’s journey through the realms of Norse mythology. Set during the apocalyptic events of Ragnarök, the game blends brutal, cinematic combat with emotional storytelling as father and son face gods, monsters, and fate itself. With expanded realms, deeper character development, and refined mechanics from its predecessor, Ragnarök delivers an epic, heartfelt finale to the Norse saga, praised for its visuals, voice acting, and masterful balance of myth and humanity.

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor is a 2023 action-adventure game developed by Respawn Entertainment and the sequel to Jedi: Fallen Order. It follows Cal Kestis, a Jedi Knight on the run from the Empire, as he battles across new planets, faces powerful enemies, and uncovers ancient secrets. With improved combat, deeper customization, and larger, more explorable environments, Survivor expands on everything that made the original a hit—blending lightsaber duels, Force powers, and rich storytelling into a thrilling and emotional Star Wars experience.

Need for Speed Unbound is a 2022 street racing game developed by Criterion Games and published by EA, blending high-octane racing with a bold, graffiti-inspired visual style. Set in the open-world city of Lakeshore, players compete in underground races, evade cops, and build their rep to enter The Grand, the ultimate street race. With deep car customization, stylized effects, and a fresh soundtrack, Unbound mixes classic NFS adrenaline with a modern, artistic twist, making it a standout in the franchise.
