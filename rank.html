Home Ranking List Pros/Cons

Elden Ring: Amazing game it allows you a lot of freedom with a ton to do, and has a fun challenging aspect to it.

God of War Ragnarok: In god of war the story of it is amazing and if you want a challenging fight they have a great difficulty settings that give you a lot of versatility in that.

Assassins Creed Shadows: This had a new style from the other recent ac games. Getting to play a Naoe reminds me of playing some of the older ac games without all the bugs that they had.

Need For Speed Unbound: This is one of the best NFS games they have put out for a while. The story in the game was really good as well.

Star Wars Jedi Survivor: The story in this game was really good but the game had a lot of frustrating mechanics in the fighting system. The mechanics made the game to be a lot harder than it really should've been, but if you are just looking for a great story this would be one of the top.
